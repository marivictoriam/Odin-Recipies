Arroz con Leche Recipe



Description

Prepare with this rice pudding, a delicious dessert that everyone loves and give it a different touch



Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water (500 ml)
  • cup of rice (250 g)
  • 1 cinnamon stick (5 g)
  • 2 cups of milk (500 ml)
  • 1/2 cup of LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK (140 g)
  • 1 cup of NESTLÉ® MILK CREAM (240 g)
  • 3 tablespoons of raisins (30 g)



Steps

    STEP 1 Mix the rice pudding ingredients

  1. In a pot over low heat, cook the water, cinnamon stick and rice for 15 minutes until it absorbs most of the liquid.


    2. STEP 2 Add the milk

  2. Add the milk and with a spoon stir constantly.


    3. STEP 3 Add the condensed milk

  3. Add the LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK and continue stirring until the rice softens and takes on a creamy consistency.


    4. STEP 4 Mix the milk cream

  4. Add the NESTLÉ® MILK CREAM and cook, stirring constantly, for 4 more minutes.


    5. STEP 5 Integrate the raisins

  5. Finally, add the raisins and stir to integrate everything. Turn off the heat and remove the pot.


    6. STEP 6 Fill the individual molds with the mixture

  6. This is a recipe that you can serve hot or cold. Pour the mixture into 8 equal portions and to decorate you can sprinkle ground cinnamon on top.
  7. Making rice pudding has never been so easy!
